Rasagulla

Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 4 cups water ,1 cup sugar

Recipe

  1. In a large pot, bring the milk to a boil. Once it starts to boil
  2. Stir the milk gently until it curdles. Let it sit for 5 minutes
  3. Line a strainer with cheesecloth and strain the curdled milk.
  4. Squeeze out the excess water from the cheesecloth .
